# Pay-Calculator-Python
This is a simple pay calculator that utilises inputted information to create an estimated pay through its calculations. Late hours are payed at 1.5x while weekend and overtime pay is at 2x. The system makes sure to consider for breaks that may be taken and deducts the pay is regards to the pay-to-minute ratio. Float() was used opposed to int() as it allowed for decimals to be used in the inputs of both the hours worked and the pay as these numbers are rarely whole numbers.
